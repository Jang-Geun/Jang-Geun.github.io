Jang-Geun Choi

Research Scientist III

Center for Ocean Engineering/Atlantic Marine Energy Center, University of New Hampshire

Community of Korean Oceanography Academic Association

I am a physical oceanographer who focuses on dynamics of geophysical costal ocean processes and also a ecosystem modeler who develops various ecological-physical coupled models. My research interest is in finding key dynamics of a system using the simplest model and theoretical approaches. I co-work with many other researchers by developing and providing the models they need.
